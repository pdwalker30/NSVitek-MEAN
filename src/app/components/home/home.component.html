The Vitek Lab

Welcome! We are a lab bridging vertebrate morphology, paleontology, and evolution with the aim of understanding how species survive on a constantly changing planet. We are members of the Department of Ecology & Evolution at Stony Brook University. Until a receded pandemic allows us to gather in-person, please imagine the lab with the same spirit and sense of adventure as O. C. Marsh's 1872 paleontological field crew, just with fewer hats.
